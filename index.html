LexSound Audio Works

Hello! I'm Alex from LexSound.
LexSound started as a YouTube channel where I upload instrumental guitar covers of genres
like K-pop, electronic music, pop, and more. I usually replicate the chord progressions of each song
and play the vocals with the guitar.
Now, LexSound has evolved into a project where I offer my services
to record electric or acoustic guitars for other artists according to their needs!
This is LexSound Audio Works, and this is my portfolio.
