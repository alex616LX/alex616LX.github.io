Services

What I can do for your music as a session guitarist.

Acoustic Guitar Recordings

Whether you need a single acoustic guitar track or want to transform your song into an acoustic version, I can arrange and record the acoustic guitars for you.

MIDI Guitars to Real Guitars

Do you have MIDI guitar tracks that feel lifeless? I can replace them with real guitar performances.

Electric Guitar Recordings

Whether you need a single riff looped for your song, want to reimagine your melodies or chord progressions on electric guitar, or explore tones from clean to distortion, I can arrange and record the electric guitars for you.

What I need to get started

To make sure I deliver exactly what your song needs, here’s the information I’ll need for before we start:

  • Your song or demo.
    A WAV or MP3 file of your song or demo.
  • Tempo information.
    BPM and whether it’s constant or has tempo changes.
  • Key / harmony details.
    Key, chord progression, scale, or any harmonic reference you’re using.
  • Arrangement references (optional but helpful).
    Any reference tracks, demos, or ideas for the guitar parts.
  • The service you need.
    Let me know which service you’re looking for (acoustic, electric, MIDI to real guitar, etc.).
